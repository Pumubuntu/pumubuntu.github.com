Pumubuntu

What is pumubuntu?



Pumubuntu is a program that helps you following the Pomodoro Technique for time management.

It is discrete and stays in the notification bar at the right top of the screen waiting for you to activate it.

It is compatible with Unity, the new Ubuntu interface introduced in it's latest versions.
